HIGH DEFINITION DIGITAL MAMMOGRAPHY

The MAMMOSCAN offers the most effective Full-Field Digital Mammography solution on the market with its low dose and high definition imaging.

HIGH DEFINITION IMAGING ENSURES ACCURATE BREAST CANCER DETECTION

With 27 microns diagnostic resolution, the MAMMOSCAN provides better visualization of the breast thus ensuring more accurate detectability of tumors and micro calcifications. Spatial resolution up to 20 line pairs per millimeter offers significant add-on value to the diagnostic process compared to other available digital mammography systems.

ADVANCED SCANNING TECHNIQUES REDUCES THE PATIENT DOSE AND INCREASES THE IMAGE HIGH DEFINITION

The MAMMOSCAN uses its patented slot-scan technology, which efficiently rejects the scattered radiation without the needs of the anti-scatter grid. The result is a reduced dose to the patient and improved resolution.

DETECTOR DESIGN RESULTS IN LESS ENVIRONMENTAL SENSITIVITY AND GREATER FLEXIBILITY OF USE

The Detector developed for the MAMMOSCAN has the great advantage of not being sensitive to high and low temperatures, this means that the system can be installed in locations that are not under permanent temperature control. This gives the users greater flexibility in where the system can be installed.

AUTOMATED EXAMINATION PROCESS INCREASES THE WORKFLOW

The motorized movements with iso-centric rotation, combined with customizable presets of the screening and diagnostic workflow significantly reduce the examination time.

FULLY AUTOMATIC EXPOSURE CONTROL SYSTEM ENSURES THE IMAGE QUALITY

The MAMMOSCAN is equipped with a fully automatic exposure control (AEC) system that analyzes the whole breast composition. This ensures proper technique factor selection, which leads to the highest image quality with the lowest possible dose.

STEREOSTATIC BIOPSY ATTACHMENT

The MAMMOSCAN is the only scanning mammography system with stereotactic biopsy capability. This is an option which converts the MAMMOSCAN into a fully functional diagnostic unit at any location. The biopsy procedure is controlled by the operator workstation, which ensures smooth automated workflow through the delicate procedure and allows the operator to keep the focus on the patient.
